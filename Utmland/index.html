UTM or Unmanned Traffic Management is a system to automate the navigation and control of drones in the air.

UTM safely integrates drones and other unmanned aerial systems (UAS) into the airspace as efficiently as possible and ensures the smooth functioning of all the operations which are autonomously controlled.

Drones can be used for missions beyond the visual line of sight (BVLOS) by employing UTM to safely navigate its route.

Conflict Management System

AirUTM is a method that can integrate human intellect with automation to reduce the chances of airborne conflicts.

Path Planning

Geofencing

Interoperability

It uses communication between aircrafts and UTM service providers for optimum safety.

Identity & Authentication
